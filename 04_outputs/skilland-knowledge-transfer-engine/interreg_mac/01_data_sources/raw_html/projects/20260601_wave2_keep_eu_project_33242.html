Description

Description (EN):
PROJECT TYPE: 3 - Governance Projects ~ MAIN INTERVENTION FIELD: 173. Enhancing institutional capacity of public authorities and stakeholders to implement territorial cooperation projects and initiatives in a cross-border, transnational, maritime and inter-regional context ~ SUMMARY: As underlined in NEXTMED synergies and complementarities document, "With a 2030 target, the EU Mission Restore our Ocean and Waters (Mission Ocean) aims to protect and restore the health of our ocean and waters through research and innovation, citizen engagement and blue investments. The Mission’s new approach will address the ocean and waters as one and play a key role in achieving climate neutrality and restoring nature". In order for Mission Ocean to reach its goals, it is necessary it is deployed uniformly and widely to help local authorities in planning and rolling out the green transition. Doing so is a real challenge for Municipalities and it is even more difficult for small ones due to a problem of lack of critical mass. Reaching the critical mass and having an appropriate transition agenda ARE THE TWO MOST URGENT NEEDS to be solved for Mediterranean Municipalities in view of the green transition and climate neutrality. TO START ANSWERING THESE NEEDS, in June 2023, the Municipality of Genova and the EU Mission Ocean launched the Coalition of Mayors for Mission Ocean and their Shared Transition Plan outlining the communalities and possible synergies among the local development plan of each municipality member of the Coalition. The aim of SHARE4MED is to FULLY ANSWER THESE NEED by consolidating the Coalition of Mayors for Mission Ocean, to expand it in EU and to extend it to non EU countries with specific focus on the WESTMED initiative. SHARE4MED EXPECTED CHANGE with respect to the current situation will be a significantly greater awareness of Municipalities on Mission Ocean and WESTMED initiatives and on their solutions and empowerment tools to be used jointly, through the common SHARE4MED action plan, in achieving the climate neutrality in their territories. MAIN OUTPUTS are the Coalition of Mayors and its Shared Agenda, Action Plan and Governance DASHBOARD. Final beneficiaries are Municipalities (TG1), local economic operators (TG3) and citizens (TG2) ~ KEEP KEYWORDS: Coastal management and maritime issues, Governance, partnership, Regional planning and development

    Contribution to wider strategies and policies:

    • Other SYNERGIES & COMPLEMENTARIETIES OF SHARE4MED WITH STRATEGIES/INITIATIVES CONTAINED IN DOC “Overview of main policies, strategies and initiatives having synergies and complementarities with Interreg NEXT MED Programme”
    • The SHARE4MED projet aims at extending to non EU Mediterranean Countries the Coalition of Majors for Mission Ocean which was launched by the Muncipality of Genova on June 2023 in the context of the EU Mission Restore our Oceans and Water by 2030 conference held in Genova.and co-organised with the WESTMED initiative. It is noted that the "Overview of main policies...." document contains multiple references to both the EU Mission (pag. 22 and 27) and the WESTMED initiative (pag, 14. 20, 25 and 37).
    • HOW THE PROPOSAL WILL CONTRIBUTE TO THE CONCERNED INITIATIVES
    • Besides being in line with and originated by these two initiatives, SHARE4MED will contribute by:
    • - making Municipalities of the project territories aware of the two strategies and raising their interest in contributing to their efforts, for instance by adhering to the Mission Ocean's Charter published by the Commission (WP2 and WP3)
    • - map each engaged Municipality's development plan (and when applicable the related Covenant of Mayors) with the Mission's Action Plan and the WESTMED initiative programme and identify common actions (WP3)
    • - assemble all these common actions into the Shared Transition Agenda of Mayors for the Mission and related short term Action Plan (WP3)
    • - develop a common and Mediterranean wide Governance Dashboard for use by the Municipalities of the Coalition to monitor the individual and common progress in achieving the Mission and WESTMED objective and to plan ahead theri future agendas as appropriate.
