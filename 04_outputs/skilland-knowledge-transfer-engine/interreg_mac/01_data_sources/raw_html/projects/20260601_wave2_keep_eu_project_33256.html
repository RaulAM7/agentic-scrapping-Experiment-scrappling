Unleashing the Power of Female Entrepreneurs in the Mediterranean

Programme 2021 - 2027 Interreg VI-B NEXT Mediterranean Sea Basin (NEXT MED)
Date of latest update: 2026-02-27

Description

Description (EN):
PROJECT TYPE: 1 - Thematic projects ~ MAIN INTERVENTION FIELD: 021. SME business development and internationalisation, including productive investments ~ SUMMARY: SheEmpower proposal stands as a beacon of opportunity within the NEXT MED program. With a focus on empowering women-led businesses, SheEmpower aims to address the joint challenges faced at a transnational level, such as limited access to resources, digitalization challenges, and the lack of entrepreneurs skills and commercialization support. SheEmpower build on three pillars Empowering Women’s Skills, Business and Innovation. The diverse and transnational consortium including chambers of commerce, universities, and business networks across Cyprus, Italy, Spain, France, Egypt and Lebanon, ensures a robust support system and proven capacity to deliver the goals. Empowering Women’s Skills through transformative change by providing entrepreneurship capacity-building, mentorship programs to enhance the competitiveness. Empowering Women’s Business through e-business and digital transformation capacity building, and SheEmpower MED e-Store creation which is dedicated to commercialize products and services. Empowering Women’s Innovation through create a network of women-led businesses, Services package to establish SMEs. Innovation Award, SheEmpower MED Network, SheEmpower MED Innovation Exhibition are tools to empower Women’s Innovation The expected results: 280 women enhancing their entrepreneurial skills, 140 female entrepreneurs receive the mentorship program; 140 women-led businesses receive Digital transformation program ; 140 female receive E-business training ; SheEmpower MED e-Store created, Up to 20 e-business and 10 women-led start-up established, Up to 100 jobs created, A network of 500 women entrepreneurs established. 21 women participate in the Business mission, Up to 20 female receive Services package to establish their own SMEs. The beneficiaries including female entrepreneurs, women-led businesses/start-up. By empowering women to become key economic actors, the project promotes inclusive economic growth, and create jobs across the MED region ~ KEEP KEYWORDS: Knowledge and technology transfer, SME and entrepreneurship

    Contribution to wider strategies and policies:

    • Other The SheEmpower proposal aligns with several key international and European strategies and initiatives, leveraging synergies to promote sustainable, inclusive economic growth and empower female entrepreneurs.
    • Synergy with UN Sustainable Development Goal 8, Promote sustained, inclusive, and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment, and decent work for all. SheEmpower enhances the competitiveness of women-led SMEs through capacity-building initiatives, contributing to inclusive economic growth in the Mediterranean. The project aims to create up to 100 jobs, particularly in Egypt, Turkey, and Lebanon, supporting full and productive employment
    • SME Strategy for a Sustainable and Digital Europe, Help SMEs transition to more sustainable and digital business models. SheEmpower synergy with initiative though offering digital transformation training and e-business capacity development, aligning with the strategy’s emphasis on digitalization.
    • COSME - Europe’s Programme for Small and Medium-sized Enterprises, Improve the competitiveness of SMEs through access to finance, markets, and support services. SheEmpower synergy with initiative through provides guidance to financing and marketing scheme and facilitate access to local and international markets through SheEmpower MED e-Store and business missions.
    • Start-Up and Scale-Up Initiative Create a better framework for start-ups to scale up. The synergy focus on support the establishment of up to 10 women-led start-ups and offers a services package to help female entrepreneurs establish SMEs or spin-offs.
    • The SheEmpower Innovation Award encourages innovation, and the project’s mentoring and coaching support help businesses scale up.
    • European Innovation Council (EIC), Support high-risk, high-impact ideas to turn science into new business opportunities. Synergies through SheEmpower Innovation Award and related support services encourage high-risk, high-impact innovations by women entrepreneurs.
