Transferring Coastal Integrated governance for sustainable tourism

Programme 2014 - 2020 INTERREG VB Mediterranean
Date of latest update: 2025-01-25

Description

Description (FR):
Afin de promouvoir le tourisme côtier durable conformément aux politiques de l'UE, COASTING PLUS s'appuie sur les efforts de COASTING pour introduire un outil de gouvernance intégrée du tourisme côtier durable dans le Méditerranéen. COASTING a abordé la fragmentation des responsabilités et politiques dans la gestion du tourisme côtier, en capitalisant un outil de gouvernance multi-niveaux (Contrat de baie) et en produisant une méthodologie commune pour mettre en place un processus participatif multisectoriel et multi-parties prenantes, conduisant à la signature d'un accord qui engage les acteurs publics/privés à mettre en œuvre une stratégie partagée.COASTING PLUS transférera ce produit dans 5 nouveaux territoires à travers la combinaison de sessions de formation et applications pratiques spécifiques au site, conduisant les partenaires destinataires et leurs associés et parties prenantes (supportés par les partenaires donateurs) à lancer processus de contrat côtier dans leurs régions.Le projet vise à 2 résultats:-au niveau local: signature de 5 protocoles (par les partenaires destinataires avec leurs associés et parties prenantes) comprenant des feuilles de route pour établir un contrat côtier pour le tourisme durable dans chaque nouvelle zone cible;-au niveau Med: signature d'un MoU pour l'adoption de la Méthodologie (par les PP destinataires), pour renouveler l'engagement à l’appliquer (par les PP donneurs), et pour promouvoir son transfert et adoption institutionnelle.

Description (EN):
In order to concretely promote sustainable coastal tourism in line with EU policies, COASTING PLUS builds upon COASTING efforts to introduce a tool for integrated governance of sustainable and responsible coastal tourism in Med area. COASTING tackled the fragmentation of responsibilities and policies in coastal tourism management, by capitalizing the experience of a multilevel governance tool (Coast Contract) and producing a common Methodology for establishing a multi-sectorial and multi-stakeholder participatory process, leading to the signature of an agreement through which public/private actors commit to implement a shared strategy. COASTING PLUS will transfer this output in 5 new territories through a transferring process combining training sessions and site-specific practical applications, which will lead the receiver partners (and their Associated partners and key stakeholders) in launching Coast Contract processes in target regions, with the technical support of giver partners.The project will achieve 2 main results: - at local level: signature of 5 protocols (by receivers with key stakeholders and APs) including site-specific roadmaps for the establishment of a Coast Contract for sustainable tourism in each new target area;- at Med level: signature of a MoU for the adoption the Methodology (by receiver PPs), for renewing the commitment for its application (by giver PPs), and to advocate for transferring and institutional uptake (by both givers and receivers).

Achievements (EN):
During this period,all partners have been actively working in the activities of the project with a permanent communication. COASTING PLUS main achievements in this period are:Activity 3.1 (Management): Three steering committee meetings in January (Midterm), March and June. FAMP has drafted two evaluation reports in March and June with cotributions of partnersActiviy 3.2 (Communication): During this period the COASTING website has been updated, the COASTING Facebook have been dynamised and two newsletters have been produced. This activity was coordinated by PP4, the partner responsible for communication, with contributions of all partnersActivity 3.3 (Horizontal Project): COASTING PLUS project participated in II MED Sustainable Tourism Convention, event organised by the Horizontal Project in Barcelona and also have been in contact with the Horizontal Angel through e-mails and online meetings. LB drafted a contribution document to the 3rd Community Building workshop organised by the horizontal project.Activity 3.4 (Dissemination): Final Conference was organized by PP1 with attendance of all partners with local stakeholders. All PPs organised a local conference in May/June in their respectives pilot areas. All PPs produced a video to show the COASTING PLUS experience in their pilot areas. Also a overall project video as produced by PP4 with contributions of all PPs.Activity 3.6 (Tailoring): LB coordinated this activity by sending the partners materials with which to develop the activity, All PPs contributed by writing their diagnosis on the pilot area in a diagnosis matrix. Based on this, a final document with recommendations for COASTING methodology aplication was drafted by LB. All partners identified good practices in sustainable tourism which were uploaded to the COASTINGWiki platform.Activity 3.7 (Training): LB and PP1 organised the training in March. LB drafted the training materials English which was after translated into five languages by PPs. Training materials in all languages were uploaded on a specific section of the COASTING PLUS website.Activity 3.8 (Roadshows): LB and PP1, as giver partners organised online tutoring meetings with receiver partners. All PPs also organised local participatory workshops in each pilot area, as a result of which each PP drafted a local protocol signed by local stakeholders. Actvity 3.9 (Take Up): LB drafted the MoU which was signed by all partners at the final conference
