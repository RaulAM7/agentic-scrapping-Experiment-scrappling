EU-MED hubs to accelerate SMEs sustainable and competitive growth

Programme 2021 - 2027 Interreg VI-B NEXT Mediterranean Sea Basin (NEXT MED)
Date of latest update: 2026-02-27

Description

Description (EN):
PROJECT TYPE: 1 - Thematic projects ~ MAIN INTERVENTION FIELD: 026. Support for innovation clusters including between businesses, research organisations and public authorities and business networks primarily benefiting SMEs ~ SUMMARY: The SPEEDUP project "EU-MED hubs to accelerate SMEs sustainable and competitive growth" is the instrument that will deliver to unemployed and disadvantaged people the mentoring, motivation and organizational services they need to set up own sustainable and competitive business in the countries Greece, Spain, Jordan, Egypt, Lebanon, Cyprus and Tunisia. The concept of the SPEEDUP project is an evolution of previous initiatives based on the implementation of a locally distributed Accelerator for different aims, such as quality and sustainability. Within SPEEDUP, the Accelerator concept will be extended to include the whole Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG) spectrum, and Green and Circular Economy as well, with a special attention to equality, inclusiveness and competitiveness. This main objective will be reached through the implementation of a network of local hubs in the project countries, acting as catalysts and clusters and uniting all the relevant stakeholders in each area that are related to entrepreneurship in the form of startups. The SPEEDUP hubs will operate as a distributed kind of “Park”, offering training/mentoring locally, in the form of jointly developed Acceleration programme. The partnership by itself will demonstratively put in place the long term development of the SPEEDUP network: applicant and partners will act as main local hubs, ensuring the financial sustainability, the local coordination and the international connection among all SPEEDUP main hubs; the associated partners will join the network as local member hubs, make use of the SPEEDUP achievements and continue fostering the SMEs sustainable growth after the end of the action; additional local actors will be encouraged to join the network and operate local member hubs within the implementation. Through its Acceleration programme, SPEEDUP will infuse sustainability issues into 210 SMEs participating to the project pilot and promote the start and competitive growth of 140 businesses. ~ KEEP KEYWORDS: Institutional cooperation and cooperation networks, Labour market and employment, SME and entrepreneurship

    Contribution to wider strategies and policies:

    • Other SPEEDUP's activities and results are addressed to contribute to different strategies/initiatives, among which the major ones are the following. > European Green Deal Strategy. The project integrates the Green Deal concepts internally, in the management of all its activities: i.e., promoting digital communication as preferred channel, reducing printing as much as possible and others. The promoted accelerator concept of the project includes the whole Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG) spectrum, and Green and Circular Economy as well. Within the sustainability topics transferred to the mentored SMEs, the training modules will include those measures to ensure sustainability transversally to any kind of activity, such as energy conservation, waste management, resource efficiency, etc. > UN Sustainable Development Goal 8, related to entrepreneurship through promoting sustained, inclusive and sustainable economic. By supporting the acceleration of SMEs starting up/innovation, the project integrates the principles and related good practices of gender equality and inclusion, which are considered pivotal aspects for competitiveness and economic sustainability. > New European Agenda for the Mediterranean. The SPEEDUP's partnership composition and objectives are built on close and continuous cooperation to ensure putting in place the actual and sustainable development of untapped potential in the partner Mediterranean project regions. The project network is set up to operate immediately and expand in time, with the ambition to continue providing support to new/innovative SMEs far beyond the project completion.
