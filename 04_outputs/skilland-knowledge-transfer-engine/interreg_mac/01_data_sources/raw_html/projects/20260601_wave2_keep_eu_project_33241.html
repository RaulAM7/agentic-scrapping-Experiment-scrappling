Description (EN):
PROJECT TYPE: 1 - Thematic projects ~ MAIN INTERVENTION FIELD: 023. Skills development for smart specialisation, industrial transition, entrepreneurship and adaptability of enterprises to change ~ SUMMARY: CERES objective is to establish Centers of Excellence (CoEs) in three project partner areas, in Greece, Palestine and Lebanon. CoEs will serve as innovation hubs, offering state-of-the-art training, fostering entrepreneurship and employment. Establishment of the CοEs will enable entrepreneurs, MSMEs, researchers, and graduates to receive interdisciplinary training from international teams of experts and obtain sufficient mentoring and technical support.The objective of the CoEs is to provide targeted capacity building that develops the skills and competencies required to meet the evolving needs of the market, especially in green and digital transition technologies. The Research Institutions (LP, P3) will develop small, flexible, online and/or physical presence courses offering cutting-edge knowledge in a specialised subject accompanied by practical experience as well for integrated support and networking to scale-up their project ideas. Practical knowledge will gain by the establishment and operation of Living Labs on smart and resilient energy solutions (WP3) with the support additionally of P1,P2,P5, for the business development of project ideas, as well as the configuration of the pilot projects (P3,WP4) to be developed by the project. Pilots will be based on different application technologies; they will embed an open-source management and supervisory system and collaborative platform, and they will be developed to be cost-effective, green and resilient (WP5). All partners will collaborate for the development of the pilot projects combining their expertise and offering a mix of state-of-the-art technology together with practical training and problem solving. WP6, clustering promotion, networking and a virtual collaborative platform and business model will ensure the sustainability of the outputs. Important aspect of CERES will be the effort to follow the NEB principles in planning and implementing the pilot projects, towards aesthetics, inclusivity, sustainabil ~ KEEP KEYWORDS: Education and training, Knowledge and technology transfer, SME and entrepreneurship

    Contribution to wider strategies and policies:

    • Other The project will build over existing structures and integrate with on-going efforts, strategies, and initiatives to enhance the impact, outreach, and alignment with broader regional objectives. The project aims to provide learning opportunities and improve the skills of MSMEs in participating countries, supporting SDG 4 by promoting education for all. It also focuses on gender equality (SDG 5), ensuring that women benefit equally from new opportunities in education, training, and employment. The project promotes new industries and innovation through the development of new technologies and practices, including RE and IoT, supporting SDG 9 by promoting industrial innovation. In addition, it addresses reducing inequalities (SDG 10) by providing equitable access to education and jobs in different sectors in the region. The transnational approach shares best practices and helps to reduce socio-economic disparities. In line with SDG 7, the project focuses on sustainable Renewable Energy-based innovative solutions, that reduce dependence on fossil fuels in rural communities and promotes environmental sustainability. This contributes to economic growth and job creation, in line with SDG 8. The project supports the EU's MSME strategy by improving MSMEs' digital and sustainable skills through training, new technologies and business models. It is in line with the EU's COSME programme, which boosts SME competitiveness through innovative solutions. Our ecosystem supports start-ups and scale-ups, fostering growth and aligning with the EU's Start-up and Scale-up initiative. Our transnational network promotes research, knowledge sharing and training across borders, supporting the objectives of Horizon Europe.
