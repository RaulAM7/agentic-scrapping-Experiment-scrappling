Description

Description (EN):
PROJECT TYPE: 1 - Thematic projects ~ MAIN INTERVENTION FIELD: 028. Technology transfer and cooperation between enterprises, research centres and higher education sector ~ SUMMARY: The HERACLES Project aims to establish and strengthen a multi-actor research network between companies and universities, focusing on the application of AI to enhance predictive medicine and health crisis preparedness, addressing significant common challenges in the Mediterranean region and transforming healthcare, making it more efficient. The project promotes Open Innovation and Open Research paradigms, exploring AI's potential alongside legal and regulatory frameworks to ensure proper valorisation of research through intellectual property and technology transfer mechanisms. Key activities of the HERACLES Project include implementing a Pilot Project that develops collaboration schemes between stakeholders at a transnational level, facilitating the exchange of researchers and entrepreneurs. These schemes aim to foster interaction between the scientific research community and the health technology business sector, integrating various stakeholders to develop AI-based tools specialised in predictive medicine and crisis management. Additionally, the project will explore and develop innovative business models to transform scientific research results into tangible commercial opportunities. By addressing common healthcare challenges through a collaborative and innovative approach, the HERACLES Project promises substantial benefits. It aims to drive forward a new era of AI-enhanced medical care, ultimately improving healthcare systems across the Mediterranean region and ensuring a more robust response to health crises. The expected changes include improved patient outcomes, more efficient allocation of healthcare resources, and the integration of advanced AI technologies into everyday clinical practice, benefiting patients, healthcare providers, and the broader medical community. ~ KEEP KEYWORDS: Health and social services, ICT and digital society, SME and entrepreneurship

Relevant linked projects:

  • PHASE - PROMOTING EHEALTH IN CB AREA STIMULATING LOCAL ECONOMIES (Italy-Albania-Montenegro 2014-2020 Program) (EN) | E-health CBC network and capacity building (web portal, B2B events, start-up competitions, workshops and international conferences) is one of the PHASE outputs. It focused on the establishment of a robust international network to promote cross-border cooperation between public and private entities, aiming to develop a cooperation model among stakeholders to enhance SMEs’ competitiveness in the involved territories. To achieve these objectives, the establishment of the network was accompanied by training activities aimed at both public entities and start-ups and enterprises, through the organisation of B2B events, start-up competitions, workshops, and international conferences. The training initiatives were a fundamental step for the success of the project, as they significantly contributed to raising awareness among the involved targets about the main thematic areas of eHealth and its potential in the entrepreneurial field.
  • The HERACLES project can benefit from and leverage the output described above, because it has demonstrated effectiveness in implementing an international network and promoting cooperation between public and private sectors. Specifically, the model of the network can be adapted and applied to the specific needs of the territories in the Mediterranean area involved in the HERACLES project, enabling effective implementation of activities and achievement of the set objectives. (EN), YESS - YOUNG ENTREPRENEURS STARTUP SCHOOL(Greece-Italy 2014-2020 Program) (EN) | 1) The CBC YESS Innovation Fund Network was created at the end of a preparation process targeting startups and startuppers and Private/Public funds and Institutions, with the aim of favouring the dialogue and the exchange of information useful to sustain innovation and economic development of the involved territories. The Innovation Fund Network offered the possibility to startups at the end of their incubation process, to present their business ideas to expert stakeholders, to receive feedback or get a round of financing. In the HERACLES project the same approach will be exploited, with appropriate adaptations, to favour the match among capitals and the stakeholders of innovation in the field of digital health and AI-driven healthcare. Same processes, documents and methods will be used to create the HERACLES network which, considering the future challenges and innovations expected in the AI domain, will also include academic representatives (universities, research centres and researchers).
  • 2) The Web incubation Platform of the YESS project aimed at providing virtual digital services to startups, big companies, public administration, and experts. It was composed of 4 different sections: Learning, Mentoring, Coaching and Challenge. In the Learning section, video lessons, texts and self-assessment quizzes were published. In the Mentor and Coaching session, the startuppers could get consultancy sessions by technical experts, or be supported by coaches. The Challenge section was dedicated to the match between Big Companies or PA needs and the technical solutions offered by startups. In the HERACLES project the Web incubation Platform will be used for the section of Learning, creating a purposely developed training content based on AI in the healthcare field targeting startups and SMEs. Also the Challenge section will be used to create a link between Healthcare Authorities or Providers and young startuppers or researchers familiar with technical AI based solutions. (EN), B4HT - Box for Health by Tradition & In-novation: promoting sustainable Mediterranean diet by Healthy Foods (funded by the PRIMA project, Section 2 – Multi-topic 2022) (EN) | The "iMFood" App is one of the B4HT outputs. It is an innovative tool designed to function as a “virtual nutritionist,” promoting healthy dietary habits based on the principles of the Mediterranean Diet. This application offers personalised dietary recommendations, helping users make healthier food choices and adopt better eating habits.
  • The next step for the "iMFood" App involves conducting a comprehensive clinical study to evaluate its health benefits in real-world settings. This study will involve a diverse group of participants using the app over a specified period, allowing researchers to gather robust data on its effectiveness in improving dietary habits and overall health outcomes. By monitoring various health metrics and dietary patterns, the study aims to provide concrete evidence of the app’s impact.
  • In the context of the HERACLES Project, the "iMFood" App will serve as a valuable data collection tool. It will gather diverse types of data, including dietary intake, health metrics, user interactions, and feedback. This data will be crucial for training an AI algorithm within HERACLES. analysing the collected data to develop advanced clinical and healthcare tools.
  • The exploitation of the "iMFood" App within the HERACLES Project exemplifies a synergistic approach, combining innovative technology with practical healthcare applications. This collaboration will not only enhance the app's functionality but also contribute significantly to the development of AI-driven healthcare solutions, ultimately benefiting users with more accurate, data-driven health recommendations and interventions. (EN), Spain Neurotech (Next Generation/ Spanish Government) (EN) | Spain Neurotech's aims to promote the consolidation of the scientific community of experts in neuroscience and associated chronic diseases.
  • It main objectives are:
  • 1) Advance developing diagnostic methods and therapies for nervous system diseases;
  • 2) Foster an ecosystem of innovation and entrepreneurship;
  • 3) Establish ethical and legal rules necessary for the application of new people-centred technologies;
  • 5) Incorporate society into scientific activities, attracting talent, and training new generations of leaders in neurotechnology.
  • The HERACLES project aligns perfectly with these objectives and aims to exploit the model used during the implementation of Spain Neurotech.
  • The knowledge generated through the first objectives will be exploited to support activities related to the development of the ICT Platform, as well as the implementation of informative campaigns targeting various stakeholders. The second objective of Spain Neurotech aligns seamlessly with the project's goal of creating a network that integrates research, innovation, and business,driving advancements in healthcare. The third objective, which involves developing ethical and legal rules for new technologies, fits perfectly with the project’s analysis of the regulatory framework for AI in the healthcare domain, as ensuring that AI-based solutions comply with ethical and legal standards is crucial for their acceptance and effective implementation in real-world healthcare settings. Lastly, the last objective of Spain Neurotech aims to attract talent, will feed the project’s activities focused on generating knowledge and awareness, by disseminating the results of the project activities. (EN), AI4HealthyAging (Spanish Government ‘AI Missions Program 2021’) (EN) | The AI4HealthyAging project was aimed at early detection of diseases derived from ageing through Artificial Intelligence. The participating entities designed and developed an intelligent solution that makes it possible to detect neurological, motor and degenerative diseases derived from ageing at an early stage, and act on them quickly. Within this intelligent system, an early prognosis is made during patient follow-up thanks to the design and construction of Artificial Intelligence algorithms.
  • The output of AI4HealthyAging project that will be exploited are:
  • 1) Databases used for training predictive and decision models;
  • 2) Pre-trained models for specific diseases or contexts, which can be further improved through new training, new databases or adapted to new diseases;
  • 3) Technological knowledge concerning the most advanced technologies that best meet the needs of tool developers.
  • Additionally, the companies and research centres that have participated in the AI4HealthyAging network can be included in the transnational research centre envisioned by the HERACLES project. This will allow them to share their experience and knowledge with other actors, promoting effective collaboration and the exchange of skills within the network. (EN), Tartaglia (Spanish Government ‘AI Missions Program 2021’) (EN) | The Tartaglia project aimed to accelerate the application of AI in the National Health System and help patients and health professionals to strengthen the prevention and early diagnosis of diseases. 16 entities were responsible for creating a federated network with Artificial Intelligence to accelerate clinical and health research in the country. Among them were agencies, foundations and research institutes linked to hospitals and regional health services, universities, spin-offs, national SMEs and large international companies. The main challenge of Tartaglia was the creation of an ecosystem that channels research activity and allows AI tools to be built on data jointly and securely. It was also sought to give greater confidence to professionals by helping them make decisions in the diagnosis and treatment of Alzheimer's, Macular Degeneration, Prostate Cancer, Complex Chronic and Ultrasound Diagnosis, creating as a result a more competitive market in Spain and Europe for encourage the creation and growth of companies.
  • The Tartaglia project fits very well and is in synergy with the HERACLES project, as they both are aimed at building a network. While Tartaglia limits its objectives to the interaction with the Spanish National System, our project has a wider perspective, also including preparedness for emergencies, and has an international perspective.
  • The Tartaglia’s outputs that will be exploited during HERACLES project are:
  • 1) Stakeholders that will be also involved in HERACLES Network, in order to exploit their capabilities acquired during the previous experiences;
  • 2) Information campaign methodologies adopted in order to disseminate goals and results will be adopted by HERACLES PPs, ensuring that the results achieved will be disseminated to as wide an audience as possible (EN), BIOPROTECH (FP7 ERAWIDE) (EN) | The BIOPROTECH project was aimed to support the CBS in the improvement of its capacities in bioprocesses development (fermentation and microarrays technologies) as well as all related fields such as technology transfer mechanisms, know-how about the EU research framework program and biosafety competencies to be better integrated in the European Research Era. The main outputs of the project that will be exploited in HERACLES project were:
  • 1) The training and recruitment opportunity in the CBS offered to three unemployed graduates from Sfax University, trained in EU project Management, technology transfer and innovation and in fermentation technology, which represented a means of contribution to job creation in Tunisia, in order to improve the territorial economic development. The HERACLES project will draw inspiration from this output, using the model proposed in the BIOPROTECH as an example and thoroughly analysing the training materials used. This approach will allow the identification of strengths in the training process that can be capitalised on during activities aimed at stakeholders.
  • 2) Training materials, which will be exploited by HERACLES to implement effective educational strategies, improve training content, and adapt teaching methodologies to the specific needs of stakeholders.
  • Furthermore, HERACLES will integrate the best practices identified, promoting continuous and high-quality training that supports the development of the skills necessary for the adoption of innovative technologies in the healthcare sector. (EN), DeepTech-2M – DeepTech Materials and Manufacturing Talent Development for an Improved EU Economy and Climate (Cohort 3,EIT HEI Initiative) (EN) | The ongoing DEEPTECH-2M Project builds up HEIs’ capacity to foster deep tech talent in order to respond to the identified importance of deep tech talent development for the future of innovation and security in Europe. The result that will be exploited in HERACLES project is:
  • 1) Capacity building experience and proven techniques aimed at fostering innovation within the EU region. This initiative will be extended to the HERACLES partner countries to tackle uneven skills distribution and stimulate the innovation ecosystem, ultimately developing world-class solutions for health challenges. The project builds upon the effective collaboration model that integrates business, higher education institutions (HEIs), research centres, and multidisciplinary efforts (including health and digital technologies). This interaction, which has been successfully tested for its effectiveness, will be harnessed to establish and support an innovation ecosystem in digital technologies, particularly focusing on AI, ML, and the Industrial Internet of Things (Industry 5.0). This comprehensive approach ensures the development of specialised talents and promotes a sustainable and secure environment for innovation in the healthcare sector. (EN), Smart2m – Innovation Capacity Building for Higher Education in Industry 4.0 and Smart Manufacturing (Cohort 1, EIT HEI Initiative) (EN) | SMART-2M project aspired to become the leading European initiative that empowers innovators and entrepreneurs to develop world-class solutions to Industry 4.0 and smart manufacturing challenges and create growth and skilled jobs. Its objectives focused on promoting and supporting institutional change in HEIs and by strengthening the integration and contribution of HEIs to innovation ecosystems, through the EIT knowledge triangle model: interaction between business, education and innovation. The output which could be exploited by the HERACLES project is:
  • 1) Ability, knowledge, experience of the successful empowerment of innovators and entrepreneurs to develop world-class solutions to existing real-world challenges and the creation of growth. This expertise also extends to researchers, enabling them to exploit their research results and transform them into viable products or services. The objectives of promoting and supporting institutional change in HEIs (affecting researchers) and strengthening the integration and contribution of HEIs (and research centres) to transnational innovation ecosystems could be applied to HERACLES project, in particular to the TN cooperation model for eHealth, by also utilising the good-practices applied through the knowledge triangle model which includes the interaction between business, education and innovation.
