Mitigating Ship Strikes and Enhancing Carbon Sequestration in the Atlantic

Programme 2021 - 2027 Interreg VI-B Atlantic Area
Date of latest update: 2026-03-20

Description

Description (EN):
The ATLANTIC WHALE DEAL aims to make sure that ships don't accidentally hit whales in the Atlantic Area. Partners from different countries are working together to prevent these collisions that can damage biodiversity, trying out new and smart technologies to reduce ship strikes. They want to help the environment by capturing carbon (which is good for the planet) and making sure the ecosystem works well. This involves localization techniques to find whales, making maps to show where there's a risk of collisions, and creating tools to reduce noise in the ocean.
Read more
Website: https://www.atlanticarea.eu/discover-our-projects/approved-projects/atlantic-whale-deal
