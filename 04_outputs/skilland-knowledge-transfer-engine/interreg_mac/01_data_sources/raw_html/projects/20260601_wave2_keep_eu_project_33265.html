HorizonCraft: Sailing towards Mediterranean expertise building

Programme 2021 - 2027 Interreg VI-B NEXT Mediterranean Sea Basin (NEXT MED)
Date of latest update: 2026-02-27

Description

Description (EN):
PROJECT TYPE: 2 - Youth-oriented projects ~ MAIN INTERVENTION FIELD: 152. Measures to promote equal opportunities and active participation in society ~ SUMMARY: HorizonCraft is a 24 months project planned by a partnership composed by 6 partners based in 3 countries; Italy, Lebanon and Egypt. The action tackles the joint challenges of the EU-MED region, particularly the growing number of young people who lack access to job opportunities. The primary issue identified is the mismatch between the skills of young people and the needs of the labor market, highlighting the need for both improved and new mechanisms to bridge this gap. The project specifically targets young people with fewer opportunities and micro, small, and medium-sized enterprises (MSMEs). It aims to answer two critical questions: What do youth want? Opportunities to learn, grow, and be integrated into the labor market. What do companies want? Prepared talent ready to join them and contribute to sustainable growth. We believe that a partnership between youth organizations and MSMEs is crucial to instill resilience in young people, equipping them with the competencies and skills needed for employment and self-employment. The main outputs can be summed up in 5 blocks: Effective outreach to the target group, leading to a good take-up rate: plans and guidelines to ensure continuity, stability and participation of participants in the project, especially youth coming from disadvantaged backgrounds. Capacity building pilots in the 3 involved territories: 1) by providing learning models and environments enabling participants to develop skills relevant for the labour market for youth and 2) Strengthening the capacity of MSMEs to mentor and support youth in their growth and development process. Matching platform: to facilitate the match of youngsters and companies based on the principle of demand/supply. Internship placement: arrangements for skills development and activation in the labour market. Policy recommendations and sustainability plans: To advocate on policy reforms and territorial cooperation across borders in the EU-MED region. ~ KEEP KEYWORDS: Education and training
